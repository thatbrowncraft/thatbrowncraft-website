✦ ✦ ✦

This space belongs to the readers. The people who showed up, kept reading, and felt things they weren't warned about. Come in.

"You kept reading through the cliffhangers, the long waits, and the chapters that wrecked you. The least I can do is actually listen."

This is the one corner of the website that's completely yours. Tell me what you loved. Tell me what you think I got wrong. Tell me which scene lives in your head rent-free at 2am. A demand, a question, a rant, a love letter to a character who doesn't know you exist. All of it is welcome here.

I do actually read these. Promise.
💌
Send a Postcard

A message to me. Could be feedback, a story request, a character demand, a general hello, or a full paragraph about why Nisarg deserved better. All categories accepted.

⭐
Leave a Review

Rated the book in your head? Put it somewhere I can actually see it. Featured reviews show up on the website. Spoiler tag available. One star ratings also accepted. I can take it.

🎨
Submit Fan Art

Drew something? Digitally painted something? Made a moodboard that captures Raghav's whole vibe in one image? Submit it. Featured pieces go up in the gallery. Full credit always given.
