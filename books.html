📕 Amodini Book 1
Amodini Series — Book 1

Amodini

Desi Romance Joint Family Arranged Marriage Slow Burn Found Family Family Saga

Three brothers. Three women who didn't sign up for any of this. One joint family that somehow contains all the chaos and all the love a household can hold.

Raghav, Nisarg, and Yug — each carrying something the others can't see — and the women who walk into their lives and refuse to let them stay the same. This is a story about how people orbit each other until they finally collide. About arranged marriages, chosen love, and the moments in between that you can't unlearn.

Plot Timeline
  • Sree steps into the Choudhary household not expecting to stay — and Raghav doesn't expect to notice her
  • Nisarg and Aadhya's quiet tension builds through every room they share and every word they don't say
  • Yug brings Devanshi home and the family is never quite the same again
  • The Carter arc — a storm that tests every bond in the house
  • Devanshi's abduction and the night that rewrites the whole story
  • What it costs to stay. What it means to choose. The ending nobody was ready for.
