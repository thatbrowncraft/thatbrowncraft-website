About the person
behind the words.

Hi. I write as thatbrowncraft because that's what this is, really. A craft. Made by someone brown. Very literally.

I grew up in Gujarat, which means I understand joint families, unsolicited chai, the chaos of festivals, and what it actually feels like when love looks like someone sliding a cup across the counter without saying anything.

I write desi romance where our culture isn't a backdrop. It's the whole thing.
