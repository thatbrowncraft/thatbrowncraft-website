Amodini Series — Book 1
R
Raghav
Lead · Amodini

"The eldest. He carries everything and tells no one about the weight."

Age 32
Occupation Architect
Chai Early mornings Silence Order
🎵 Theme: Tum Se Hi — Jab We Met
Personality

Raghav doesn't explain himself. He doesn't need to. He shows up, handles things, and then goes quiet. The problem is that nobody ever asks him how he's doing, because from the outside it always looks like he's doing fine. He's not. He just got very good at making it look that way.

Dislikes
Dishonesty Loud spaces Being pitied Asking for help
Trivia
  • Makes chai exactly the same way every single morning. Same cup, same amount of time.
  • He noticed Sree before he admitted it, even to himself.
  • Has never once asked anyone in the family for something he needed.
Author's Note

Raghav was the one I understood before I wrote a single word about him. The eldest child who becomes quietly responsible for everyone else before anyone thinks to ask if that's what he wanted. He's not tragic. He's just very, very tired. And then Sree shows up.

S
Sree
Lead · Amodini

"Quiet until she isn't. She notices everything and says very little until it matters."

Age 28
Occupation Researcher
Books Rain Old films Quiet rooms
🎵 Theme: Ae Dil Hai Mushkil
Personality

Sree takes up exactly as much space as she decides to. Which is usually very little, until the moment she decides otherwise. She has strong opinions she keeps mostly to herself, a very dry sense of humour that she deploys at unexpected moments, and a way of making people feel seen that she doesn't realise she has.

Dislikes
Being patronised Pretence Forced conversations
Trivia
  • She walked into that house not expecting to stay. She didn't plan on Raghav either.
  • Has a habit of finishing other people's sentences in her head and never saying them out loud.
  • The one who notices when something in the house changes. Usually before anyone else does.
Author's Note

Sree is the character I would be most afraid to disappoint. She deserved a love story that respected her quietness, not one that tried to fix it. She's not waiting to become louder. She's just waiting for someone who knows how to listen at her volume.

N
Nisarg
Lead · Amodini

"Says the least. Means the most. Every word he speaks, he meant three conversations ago."

Age 30
Occupation Doctor
Precision Late nights Aadhya's laugh
🎵 Theme: Agar Tum Saath Ho
Personality

Nisarg has exactly three ways of saying I love you, and none of them are those words. He shows up. He pays attention. He remembers things nobody asked him to remember. That's it. That's all of him. And somehow it's everything.

Dislikes
Small talk Wasted time Being misread
Trivia
  • Has not raised his voice in an argument. Not once. This terrifies people more than shouting would.
  • Knows Aadhya's coffee order even though she never told him.
  • The middle brother. Somehow the most overlooked and the most observant at the same time.
Author's Note

Nisarg was never supposed to be this complicated. He was just going to be the quiet middle brother. And then he refused to be anything less than a whole person with a whole inner world, and here we are.

A
Aadhya
Lead · Amodini

"She already knew. She waited anyway. The patience of someone who has decided what she wants."

Age 27
Occupation Architect
Architecture Sunrises Planning Idhayan
🎵 Theme: Raataan Lambiyan
Personality

Aadhya is not patient in the way people assume she is. She's not passive. She's not waiting because she has no other options. She's waiting because she's already made her decision and she's giving Nisarg the space to catch up. There's a difference. A huge one.

Dislikes
Indecision Being misread as passive Vague answers
Trivia
  • Loves Idhayan with her whole chest and he loves her back with equal chaos.
  • Always arrives five minutes early. Always.
  • Has already designed the home she wants to live in. She just hasn't told anyone yet.
Author's Note

I wanted to write a woman who knows exactly what she wants and is not apologetic about wanting it. Aadhya is not waiting to be chosen. She's choosing, and she's waiting for him to catch up with the choice she already made. That's not the same thing at all.

Y
Yug
Lead · Amodini

"The youngest. Still figuring out what kind of person he's going to be. Doing it anyway."

Age 25
Occupation Filmmaker
Films Devanshi Idhayan Spontaneity
🎵 Theme: Kesariya
Personality

Yug is the one everyone underestimates because he's youngest and because he laughs too much. But here's the thing about Yug. He pays attention. He saw Devanshi before he fell for her, actually saw her, and that's different from most people who look but don't see.

Dislikes
Helplessness Being underestimated Silence that isn't peace
Trivia
  • Has a film reference for every situation. Deploys them at completely the wrong moments.
  • The one who noticed Devanshi was struggling before she said anything.
  • Idhayan's favourite person in the house by a very large margin.
Author's Note

Yug grew up in the writing. He started lighter and got heavier as the story demanded it of him. He rose to it. That surprised me more than it probably should have.

D
Devanshi
Lead · Amodini

"Survived more than you know. Built herself back from things that would break most people."

Age 24
Occupation Interior Designer
Design Colour Safety 3AM with Yug
🎵 Theme: Phir Le Aya Dil
Personality

Devanshi carries things quietly. Not because she has to, but because she learned early that nobody really wanted to know the weight. Then she walked into this house and found people who actually asked. That took the longest time to get used to. Someone just asking, and meaning it.

Dislikes
Enclosed spaces Being reduced Pity without action
Trivia
  • Redesigned her bedroom three times in two years. It's her way of reclaiming space.
  • Cannot sleep in total silence. Yug figured this out before she told him.
  • The strongest person in the book. She just doesn't perform it.
Author's Note

Devanshi is the one I was most protective of while writing. I didn't want her survival to be her whole identity. She survived, yes. She also designs beautiful rooms, loves the colour green, laughs at Yug's terrible film references, and makes the whole house feel different when she's in it. Both things are true.

I
Idhayan
Supporting · Amodini

"Toddler chaos. The purest energy in the house. Everyone's favourite person."

Age 2
Occupation Professional Chaos Agent
Yug mama Biscuits Causing scenes
🎵 Theme: Lakdi Ki Kathi
About

Idhayan does not know that he is holding the entire emotional centre of this family together simply by existing. He just knows that Yug mama will always catch him when he jumps from the sofa, and that Aadhya didi gives the best biscuits, and that the whole house smells like home.

Trivia
  • Called Yug "baba" once by accident. Yug did not correct him immediately.
  • Has a specific biscuit preference and will reject the wrong brand with full toddler energy.
  • Every adult in the house has a slightly different voice they use only for him.
Author's Note

Idhayan was never planned. He just showed up in a scene and the whole house rearranged itself around him. That's exactly what toddlers do.
